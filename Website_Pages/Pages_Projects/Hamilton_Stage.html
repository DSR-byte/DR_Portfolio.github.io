I designed and built a mechanical stage simulator inspired by the rotating platform used in the musical Hamilton.
The system incorporated custom PCB design, Arduino programming, and 3D-printed components, along with an IR-based control interface for precise rotational control.
This project was presented at a student showcase, where it received one of the highest evaluation scores among all entries.
