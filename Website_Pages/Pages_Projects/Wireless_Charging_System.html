Wireless Charging System - Working Prototype

Built a small-scale wireless power transfer prototype utilizing LTSpice for circuit simulation, MATLAB for analysis, and Fusion 360 for mechanical design.
This project allowed me to apply newly introduced concepts in power transfer and regulation, bridging theoretical knowledge with practical experimentation at the component level.
I was able to successfully charge a battery wirelessly from a power supply, which made the results especially rewarding and exciting to demonstrate.
