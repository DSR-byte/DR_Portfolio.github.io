Welcome! I'm glad you're here. This site has everything you need to know about me—from my background to my projects.


I designed and built this website myself to share my work, ideas, and passions in one place. Take a look around!
Attending Cal Poly has equipped me with a unique set of skills that stand out in the industry. The university's "Learn by Doing" philosophy has allowed me to gain hands-on experience with tools such as oscilloscopes, function generators, and digital multimeters. I am proficient in industry-standard simulation tools like MATLAB, LTSpice, Fusion 360, Eagle, and more.

These lend to my comfort in the design and manufacturing process, enabling me to take ideas from concept to reality efficiently. I am also adept at troubleshooting and optimizing designs to ensure they meet both functional and aesthetic requirements. I have developed strong problem-solving abilities, teamwork skills, and a commitment to continuous learning, which I bring to every project I undertake.
I have over 6 months of industry experience in the world of startups and over two years of experience with common lab equipment. I also have 5 months of experience with high-power equipment such as high-power induction motors and 3-phase power electronics like 3-phase inductors, etc.

Additionally, I have worked on projects that required designing and testing power systems, troubleshooting complex circuits, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to meet project deadlines. My hands-on experience has given me a strong foundation in electrical engineering principles and practical applications.
My philosophy aligns closely with the definition of engineering as "the application of science and math to solve real-world problems within constraints." I take on challenges directly and focus on delivering practical, effective solutions.

In team environments, I aim to both listen actively and contribute insight with clarity and purpose. I believe in collaboration models like shared leadership—where expertise guides decisions, not hierarchy—and constructive conflict, where differing views drive better outcomes, not friction. Above all, I work to maintain alignment on goals while challenging assumptions. As I see it, “The only constant in comparing success is knowing that you gave it your all.” That’s the standard I hold myself to—regardless of the outcome.
