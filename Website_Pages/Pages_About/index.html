Welcome! I'm glad you're here. This site has everything you need to know about me—from my background to my projects.


I designed and built this website myself to share my work, ideas, and passions in one place. Take a look around!
Cal Poly’s “Learn by Doing” approach gave me hands-on experience with tools like oscilloscopes, function generators, and digital multimeters.

I'm proficient in MATLAB, LTSpice, Fusion 360, Eagle, and other industry tools, which makes me comfortable taking designs from concept to prototype.

I troubleshoot effectively, optimize for function and form, and bring strong problem-solving, teamwork, and a drive to keep learning.
I have over 6 months of industry experience in the world of startups and over two years of experience with common lab equipment. I also have 5 months of experience with high-power equipment such as high-power induction motors and 3-phase power electronics like 3-phase inductors, etc.

Additionally, I have worked on projects that required designing and testing power systems, troubleshooting complex circuits, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to meet project deadlines. My hands-on experience has given me a strong foundation in electrical engineering principles and practical applications.
My philosophy aligns with the core of engineering: applying science and math to solve real-world problems within constraints. I take on challenges directly and focus on delivering practical, effective solutions.

In team environments, I listen actively and contribute insight with clarity and purpose.

To me, the only constant in measuring success is knowing you gave it your all. That is the standard I hold myself to, no matter the outcome.
